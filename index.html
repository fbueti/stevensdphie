About Us

Welcome to the Epsilon Gamma Chapter of Delta Phi Epsilon! The sisters of Delta Phi Epsilon pride themselves in promoting love, friendship, and loyalty among each other and the people around them. A sister encourages one to be true to who you are. "Esse quam videri." To be rather than seem to be. This is the upholding quality and standard of a Delta Phi Epsilon sister.
